Some things about me. (Kind of how my life has gone so far.) I was born September 7th, 1989 in Moscow, Russia. In August of 1996, me and my family immigrated overseas and started living the American Dream in Brooklyn, NY. In 2001, we moved to Rockland County - Suffern, NY to be exact. Around this time, I developed a deep love for guitar and songwriting. After finishing High School there, I started going to RCC (Rockland Community College). At the same time I was the lead vocalist in a post-hardc0re band called Vital Limit. After we broke up, and during the Occupy movement of 2011, I packed my bag and traveled the country by myself mostly by bus (at other times by train, truck, and once by 16-wheel semi). After making it all the way to Los Angeles and having had achieved what I set out to do, I bought a plane ticket home. I moved to New Paltz Village in 2012 to continue college at New Paltz University, and in May of 2016 I had finished my Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science. After that, and due to my love of California, I drove across the country on a beat up 1996 Dodge Escort on $700. I started fixing phones at the Carlsbad Mall, got an apartment and started a new life. After covid left me homeless on Venice beach (and trust me, I loved every minute of living in my car on the beach), I moved to Milwaukee, WI for a better quality of life (you know: apartment, hot water, shower, etc.) which is where I reside now. I have many new projects in the works for you all so stay tuned! Love, DM
